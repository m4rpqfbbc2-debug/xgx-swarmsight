# XDX LICENSING PLAYBOOK

## UAE-Based Global Software Licensing & Distribution

---

## 1. ENTITY OVERVIEW

### 1.1 What XDX Is

XDX is a UAE-based software licensing company that owns intellectual property rights to a portfolio of vertical SaaS products and distributes them globally through territory-based equity partnerships.

**Registered:** Dubai, UAE (trading name of the licensing entity)

**Core function:** Own IP → License to territories → Collect revenue → Pay commissions → Enforce exclusivity

**Not:** A software development company (that's XGX UK), a private equity firm, or a traditional SaaS vendor.

### 1.2 Why Dubai

- Tax efficiency on licensing revenue
- Neutral jurisdiction for global distribution
- Strong IP protection framework
- No restrictions on profit repatriation
- Growing hub for international business

### 1.3 The Business Model in One Sentence

XDX licenses software products to territory-based partners who commit MRR in exchange for equity ownership, creating a capital-light global distribution network where partners are incentivised to grow the platform because they own part of it.

## 5. THE LICENSING DISTRIBUTION MODEL (LDM)

### 5.1 Core Mechanic

Each product in each territory has **5 equity slots** available. Partners purchase slots to earn equity and commit MRR.

| Slots | Equity | MRR Commitment | Notes |
|-------|--------|----------------|-------|
| 1 slot | 10% | £10k/month | Minimum buy-in |
| 2 slots | 20% | £20k/month | |
| 3 slots | 30% | £30k/month | |
| 4 slots | 40% | £40k/month | |
| 5 slots | 50% | £50k/month | Maximum — secures exclusivity |

**Key rules:**
- 5 slots available per product, per territory (100 territories per product)
- Each slot = 10% equity for £10k MRR commitment
- Partners take minimum 1 slot, maximum 5 slots
- Multiple partners can share slots (e.g., three partners take 2, 2, 1 slots = 5 slots filled)
- First-committed basis — early movers get priority
- Partner taking all 5 slots = exclusive territory rights for that product
- **5-year contract term** on all commitments
- Equity is per-product, per-territory (a partner can hold 3 slots in Hirestack CRM and 1 slot in Onsyte)

### 5.2 Revenue Flow

**Baseline revenue (first £50k MRR per product per territory):**
- 100% flows to LicCo
- Partner earns equity position but no cash commission on baseline

**Incremental revenue (MRR above £50k per product per territory):**
- Partner earns commission = their equity %
- Remainder flows to LicCo

**Example:**
```
Partner: 30% equity in Hirestack CRM, UK territory
Territory MRR: £80k (£50k baseline + £30k incremental)

Partner commission: 30% × £30k = £9,000/month
LicCo receives: £50k (baseline) + £21k (70% of incremental) = £71k/month
```

### 5.3 TerritoryCo Economics

TerritoryCos retain **zero operating margin**. They exist only to:
- Hold equity positions
- Hold partner agreements
- Provide legal structure for territory-based ownership

All cash flows through to LicCo. Commission payments go from LicCo directly to partners.

### 5.4 What Partners Get

| Benefit | Timing |
|---------|--------|
| **Better product** | Immediate — replace legacy systems |
| **Operational improvement** | Immediate — AI capabilities, modern UX |
| **Equity ownership** | Immediate — stake in territory pool |
| **Commission income** | Ongoing — once baseline is exceeded |
| **Exclusivity** (if 50%) | Immediate — protected territory position |
| **Exit upside** | Long-term — AI multiples (15–30× ARR) vs. traditional SME multiples (3–7× EBITDA) |

### 5.5 What Partners Commit

| Obligation | Detail |
|------------|--------|
| **Slot commitment** | Minimum 1 slot (£10k MRR), maximum 5 slots (£50k MRR) |
| **Contract term** | 5 years |
| **Performance threshold** | Maintain committed MRR or face clawback |
| **Platform exclusivity** | Cannot resell competing products in same vertical |
| **Customer onboarding** | Responsible for introducing customers; XGX UK handles implementation |

## 9. PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT

### 9.1 Partner KPIs

| Metric | Definition | Threshold |
|--------|------------|-----------|
| **Committed MRR** | Contracted monthly amount | Per agreement |
| **Actual MRR** | Current monthly revenue | Must meet committed |
| **Activation rate** | Customers live ÷ customers signed | ≥80% within 90 days |
| **Net retention** | MRR retained + expansion − churn | ≥95% monthly |
| **Customer NPS** | End-customer satisfaction | ≥40 |

### 9.2 Clawback Triggers

| Trigger | Consequence |
|---------|-------------|
| MRR falls below 80% of commitment for 3 consecutive months | Cure notice issued |
| MRR falls below 50% of commitment | Immediate review, potential equity reduction |
| Breach of exclusivity (reselling competitor) | Equity forfeiture |
| Material contract breach | Agreement termination, equity forfeiture |
